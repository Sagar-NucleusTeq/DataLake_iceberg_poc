# Lead Source Originator
Identifies whether a given lead source is managed by AAA Life, or by a club.

### Examples
| Lead Source | Originator |
|-------------|------------|
| Paid Search | AAA Life   |
| Email Club  | Club       |
| Referrals   | Club       |
| Video Ad    | AAA Life   |
